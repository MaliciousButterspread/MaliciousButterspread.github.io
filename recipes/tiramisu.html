Tiramisu
01
Prepare your bowl, and put 240g of Cream Cheese. Also add 100g of Whipped Cream and prepare a mixing tool.
If applicable, add some sugar for the sweet taste of Tiramisu.
02
After complete mixing, prepare bread material to form the base.

(If applicable, dip bread in espresso before placing!)
03
I have used a mini croissant for substitute!
Place the bread material in the container, in a nice row, forming a layer.
Also, if you have not dipped the bread in espresso yet, use a tea spoon to spread it
over the bread!
04
Now you spread the cream mixture over the bread layer!
Try to cover it evenly, too much cream will break the layer and make it ugly!
05
Now here goes my kick!
Grind an orange (just a peel)
over the cream layer!
It adds a citrus scent
and unique flavor to the cream!
06
Tip: You can use any citrus fruit for this step!
BUT, try not to grind all the way to the white part. It will add a bitter taste, ruining the scent!
07
Repeat the bread material and coffee soaking process again!
In this case, you have to squeeze the bread before you place it over the cream layer.
08
Now repeat it until the container is fully filled!
Also don't forget about the citrus kick! Finish it with a cocoa powder(or hot chocolate mix)!
09
Now you Have It!
But not so quick! You need to chill it in the fridge for an hour or two!
Make sure the bread soaks the espresso evenly in the fridge, so it can give you the best flavor!
