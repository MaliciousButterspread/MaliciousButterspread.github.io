Panna Cotta
01
Before we start, we are going to prepare a Gelatin Mixture.
Add 1 packet (usually 7 to 8g) of gelatin powder to 50ml of warm water, and mix it well.
The photo has more water since it is for larger portion. It is usually a quarter of that amount!
02
After complete mixing, prepare a bowl and be ready for some heat! Put all the sugar and cream into the bowl, and heat it up around 70 to 80 degrees Celsius.
Do not boil it, it is the key of this recipe!
03
Mix it under a thourough heat!
Make sure there are no bubbles rising from the mixture. If you want some extra flavour, you can add a Maple Syrup as you want while heating!
04
Now while heating, add two table spoon of Vanilla Extract!
After that, stir until the sugar desolves in the cream all the way down.
Not remaining a single sugar particle helps with smooth texture later!
05
After heating, mix it with the gelatin mixture we have made moment ago!
Make sure to mix it thouroughly while pouring the mixture!
06
Now for the fun part!
Hold the berry you prefer in your hand above the bowl.
Be careful! Since the mixture is hot, try to avoid holding it from high above.
07
SQEEZE!!!
The berry juice should flow into the mixture, adding flavor and the berry adds unique texture as well!
08
Now you cool down the Panna Cotta in the fridge for over 4 hours!
It allows the Gelatin mixture to harden up and cool down the heated up cream, making a perfect Panna Cotta!
